Recipe 1: Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 Tablespoon white sugar
  • ¼ Teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 ¼ Cups milk
  • 3 Tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 Egg

Directions

  1. Mix together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients.
  3. Add milk, melted butter, and egg into the well.
  4. Stir everything together until the batter is smooth.
  5. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat.
  6. Pour or scoop about 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look dry.
  8. Flip the pancake and cook until the other side is browned.
  9. Repeat the process with the remaining batter.

Recipe 2: Grilled Salmon

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds salmon fillets
  • Lemon pepper to taste
  • Garlic powder to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • ⅓ Cup soy sauce
  • ⅓ Cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ Cup water
  • ¼ Cup vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Season salmon filets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.
  2. Stir together soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil in a bowl until sugar dissolves.
  3. Place the seasoned fish in a large resealable plastic bag.
  4. Pour the soy sauce mixture into a ziplock bag.
  5. Place the fish pieces inside the bag, ensuring they are fully coated with the marinade.
  6. Seal the bag and leat it marinate
  7. Refrigerate the bag for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to infuse.
  8. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  9. Remove the salmon from the marinade and discard the excess marinade.
  10. Place the salmon filets on the preheated grill.
  11. Cook the salmon for about 6 to 8 minutes on each side, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.

Recipe 3: Flank Steak

Ingredients

  • ½ Cup vegetable oil.
  • ⅓ Cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • ¼ Cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 Tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 ½ Tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 Cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ Teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (1 1/2-pound) flank steak

Directions

  1. Mix oil, soy sauce, vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic, and pepper for marinade.
  2. Place steak in a dish, pour marinade over it, cover, and refrigerate for 2 to 12 hours.
  3. Preheat grill, remove steak from marinade, discard excess.
  4. Grill steak for 5 minutes per side.
  5. Let steak rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

Recipe 4: Strawberry Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups mashed fresh strawberries
  • 2 Cups whole milk
  • 2 Cups heavy cream
  • 1 Cup white sugar
  • 2 Teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¼ Teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Mix strawberries, milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, salt, and food coloring in a large bowl.
  2. Pour the mixture into the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker.
  3. Freeze following the manufacturer's instructions.
  4. Transfer the frozen mixture to an airtight container and freeze until firm.
